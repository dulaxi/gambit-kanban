Why the archived banner uses honey, not soft-lime like other filter pills

Every other filter pill in the bar (Sort, Priority, Assignee, Label, Due) became soft-lime during the selected-state migration — meaning "I just toggled this filter on." Archived stayed honey because it's not really a filter; it's a category switch — "show me cards from a different state of the world (archived)." Honey reads as "warning / historical" rather than "actively filtering current view."

If you decide later that visual coherency outweighs that semantic split, the swap is two-line — replace the honey classes with soft-lime. Or you can keep honey but tokenize the two hardcoded hex codes. Both fixes are under 10 minutes; saved for whenever you want to revisit.
