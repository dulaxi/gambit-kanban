Card label color experiments

Same card data, eight label-style variants. Pick one (or mix two) — I’ll wire it into Card.jsx:90-92.

A · current — bare /text
B · LABEL_BG · full pill
C · LABEL_BG_QUIET · 60% bg
D · color text only · /text

G · outline pill · border only

Quick read:

  • A is what ships now. Lowest signal — labels invisible at a glance.
  • B / C highest signal but adds a lot of color noise. Risk: the card starts looking like a fruit salad with 3+ labels.
  • D minimal change from A; just colors the text. Subtle, restrained, scannable.
  • E dot prefix — color signal lives in a 6px dot, text stays neutral. Reads as a “tag legend.”
  • F slash colored — preserves the “/text” aesthetic exactly while adding color signal where the eye already lands first.
  • G outline pill — distinctive but heavier than D/F.
  • H underline — quiet, similar to D in feel.
