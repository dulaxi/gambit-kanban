Width Drawer most coherent

The pill block animates max-width from 0 → expanded. Pills reveal left-to-right as the drawer opens. The surrounding buttons (Share/Sort/Filter) glide LEFT continuously because the flex row reflows during the width interpolation — no separate animation needed.
